# Problem statement and design scope

## HMW

How might we help students safely and honestly document, connect, and meaningfully reflect on their school experiences (e.g. CCA, ECG experiences) to grow (e.g. identity, purpose, relationships, choices) in a self-driven and accessible way?

This scope informs prototyping and exploration. It can narrow or shift based on what we learn.

## Likely in scope

- Finding safeguards to prevent burden of knowledge on the teacher and prevent excessive workload
- Helping students understand their own narrative to themselves over time
- CCA as a critical anchor and starting point, but likely not the only entry

## Likely not in scope

- Narrative presentation: how students show and share their story with others (e.g. scholarship application)

## Definitely not in scope

- Replacing the FSBB calculator tool (its own ongoing workstream)
- Identifying students who might need additional psychological help
