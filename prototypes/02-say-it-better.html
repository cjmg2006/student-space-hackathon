Say It Better

A voice-rant-to-articulation tool that gives you back your own meaning, more clearly.

How it works

You ramble about something that happened. The app returns three drafts: as you said it, as a claim about you, as a story. Pick the truest one, edit, save. The library compounds into a private dictionary of self.

The magic moment

The third version lands and you think "yes, that's what I meant, but I couldn't say it."

Anchored to

H1 (the gap is linguistic, not experiential). Naming is the upstream act of identity. Self-perception, advocacy, decision-making all flow from articulation.
