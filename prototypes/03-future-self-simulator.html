CONCEPT 03

Future Self Simulator

Step into a possible future. Live a Tuesday in it. Make choices. See how it goes.

How it works

Three ways to step in.

Try a Day — watch a Tuesday in a possible future unfold. Pure observation with subtitles. At day's end, pick the moment that lit you up.

Talk to Future You — a 5-turn chat with an older version of yourself. They tell you about their day, then offer you a postcard.

Branching Path — name a fork (subject combo, JC vs poly, CCA leadership). See two 3-year futures side by side. Pick which feels more like you.

The magic moment

Future-you turns to face you in the lamplit studio at 8:30 PM and asks the real question.

Anchored to

H3 (hard to see what's possible) + H12 (action gap). The gap between "I can't see a future" and "one tiny thing to try" is the highest-leverage intervention in the problem space.

Try the demo Three modes to try. Try a Day: watch the music producer Tuesday, pick the vocalist moment at debrief. Talk to Future You: have a chat, accept the postcard at the end. Branching Path: pick subject combo, read both, pick one.
