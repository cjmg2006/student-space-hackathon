CONCEPT 01

Mirror Talk

A mobile ritual where friends ask each other one good question and reflect each other back, asynchronously.

How it works

You pick friends who saw you today. They each get one question about you. They reply privately, in their own time. Their mirrors arrive in your library, sometimes hours later.

The magic moment

A new mirror lands with a soft chime of light, and you see yourself through someone else's eyes for the first time.

Anchored to

H4 (friends shape 40-50% of self-knowledge), H10 (fear of consequences). The disclosure surface is closed-friend-group, not institutional.

Try the demo Tap Send tab → pick 2 friends → confirm. Then Pending → reply to Tasha (try Emoji, Scene, or Dial mode). Wait 4s on the Mirrors tab for arrival.
