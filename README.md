# Student Space (hackathon project)

MOE hackathon project. Design concept to test with students around safe, honest reflection on school experiences (CCA, ECG) for personal growth.

## Status

- Phase: ideation, prepping concepts to test with students
- Started: 2026-04-27
- Source research: pen-and-paper synthesis from school visits in April 2026 (NYGH and others)
- Delivery: mobile phone app for Sec 1-4 students

## Key constraints (from synthesis)

- **Critical risk:** consequence fear vs authentic sharing. If students do not trust the container is safe, no mechanism produces honest signal.
- **Covertness paradox:** if labelled as reflection, students disengage. If hidden, transparency suffers.
- **Relational by default:** standalone digital tools (Journey Together, SkillsFuture portal) failed because students are relational. Tool needs a human activation layer.
- **Action gap:** understanding self does not auto-produce next steps. Bridging both may be needed.

## HMW

> How might we help students safely and honestly document, connect, and meaningfully reflect on their school experiences (e.g. CCA, ECG experiences) to grow (e.g. identity, purpose, relationships, choices) in a self-driven and accessible way?
